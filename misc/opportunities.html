Technology Opportunities

Our group has decided to focus on two specific aspects of data assurance:

Communication of Data:

This section will examines the process by which data reaches its intended destination while maintaining data assurance principles such as security and quality.
Thanks to our researches, we will be able to better understand the mechanism that help us to secure the data

Security of Communication:

This section comprehend all the practices implemented to protect digital information from unauthorized access or theft throughout the lifecycle of the data being transfer.
Within our group, we aim to understand the importance of data security and explain into details some the different strategies that are taken into place to achieve it.
