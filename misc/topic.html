Chosen Technology/Topic

Why Data Assurance?

The topic of data assurance was chosen due to it undeniable importance in today’s world, where, the accessibility and integrity of the data exchange through the network play a critical role.
Our research aims to shed light on this area by digging into its significance and justifying its selection.

In today’s society, access data with trust has become more and more important day by day.
Ensuring data integrity is vital to secure sensitive information from falling into wrong hands
( as demonstrated by the notorious Yahoo hacking accident in August 2013, where a hacking group exposed a staggering 3 billion accounts ).
